# Design Context

## Users
This portfolio serves **multiple audiences**: potential clients/employers evaluating your work, general creative audience exploring your portfolio, and peers/designers looking for collaboration or inspiration. Each visitor should leave with a clear sense of your capabilities and approachable, capable personality.

## Brand Personality
**Human-centered, approachable, playful**

The site intentionally balances refinement with personality. The hero's "Human. Minimal. Deliberate." messaging—with interactive weight shifts and the wiggling letter—reveals the exact aesthetic: human touches (imperfect, playful) paired with minimal, deliberate design. The experimental theme toggle reinforces that this is a creative, explorative space.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Refined minimalism with intentional moments of personality and motion
- **Core principle**: Whitespace and typography carry the design; motion and micro-interactions add warmth
- **References**: Swiss design (grid-based, clear hierarchy) meets playful expressivity (the wiggle, the experimental theme)
- **Anti-references**: Nothing glossy, no unnecessary glass-morphism, no generic AI aesthetics
- **Theme**: Light-mode primary (clean, professional) with an experimental dark theme toggle (purple/lime)

## Design Principles
1. **Intentionality over decoration** — Every element should serve a purpose or create meaningful delight
2. **Type and space first** — Typography hierarchy and generous spacing do the heavy lifting
3. **Warmth through motion** — Subtle, purposeful animations (not over-animated) make the interface feel human
4. **Accessible playfulness** — Interactive moments are delightful but never confusing; never obscure critical information
5. **Respect reduced motion** — All animations have graceful no-motion fallbacks

---

## Polish Priorities
- Visual alignment and spacing consistency
- Typography refinement (hierarchy, line-length, widows/orphans)
- Complete interaction state coverage (hover, focus, active, disabled)
- Micro-animation smoothness and appropriateness
- Content/copy polish and consistency
- Responsive behavior at all breakpoints
- Accessibility verification (WCAG AA, focus indicators, color contrast)
