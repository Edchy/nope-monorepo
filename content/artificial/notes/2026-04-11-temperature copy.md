---
title: Temperature isn't creativity, it's confidence
date: 2026-04-11
tags: [llms, mental-models]
---

Low temperature doesn't make a model "dumb" — it makes it more decisive. High temperature doesn't unlock hidden knowledge; it just makes the model hedge less and commit more wildly to whatever direction it starts going.

The useful mental model: temperature controls how far the model is willing to stray from the most obvious next token. Creativity comes from the training data, not the dial.
