One of those useful reminders: model choice matters more than people want it to. I had a problem I kept circling with Sonnet, switched to Opus, and it was basically over in two shots.

That doesn't make Sonnet bad. It just means "good enough most of the time" and "the one that actually gets you unstuck" are not the same category.
