`/insights` is cool because it turns the codebase itself into feedback. Instead of waiting to notice patterns manually, it surfaces recurring issues, opportunities, and places where the repo is trying to tell you something.

It feels less like a command and more like a regular maintenance habit. Run it often enough and the project gets easier to steer.
